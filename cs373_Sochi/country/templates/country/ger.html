Germany

Georg Hackl won three consecutive gold medals in the men's single luge at the 1992, 1994 and 1998 Olympic Winter Games. Hackl was also the first luge competitor to have won medals in five consecutive winter Olympic competitions, having finished with silver at the 2002 Salt Lake Games and also a silver at the 1988 Calgary Games when competing for West Germany.

Speed skater Gunda Niemann-Stirnemann [nee Kleeman] won eight career winter Olympic medals, including gold in the women's 3000m and 5000m at the 1992 Albertville Games and gold again in the women's 3000m at the 1998 Nagano Games.
